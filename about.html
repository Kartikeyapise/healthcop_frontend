ABOUT US

Healthcop provides India’s most comprehensive, authentic and affordable healthcare services at K-12 schools.

We are a group of young dynamic and passionate professionals from premiere institutions of India like BJ Medical College , IIT Bombay and NIMHANS Institute of Mental Health. We joined hands in 2019 and formed Healthcop with an aim of making all schools in India Health promoting School.

MOTIVATION

Out of 1.3 billion Indians, 50% are below 25 years of age.

Despite being the youngest nation, India continues to have long standing endemic problems like anemia and is constantly hit by newer lifestyle diseases like obesity.

A shocking and disheartnening truth: Globally, India stands 2nd in childhood obesity,1st in teenage depression and is in high order in many other diseases.
At Healthcop we believe schools are the best settings to provide positive impacts on children’s health.

VISION

To make 1 lac kids and their families healthier by 2022

MISSION

To empower 1.5 million Indian schools to become a Health promoting school
