COVID 19 SHIELD

An end to end medicolegally compliant service product for healthy & safe re opening of schools.

What Is the Problem?

Schools are the ones that will be the hardest to reopen because it is centered around children.
With children, the involvement of teachers, faculty and healthcare workers is vital to establish a COVID free environment In several countries like US Israel, there was a massive spike in cases among child population post reopening, despite taking precautions

The HealthCop Solution

We are HealthCop a team of professionals from BJ Medical College, IIT Bombay, and experts from several fields, and we have designed a product specially catering to the dynamic needs of a reopening school in a country like ours. It is the most comprehensive, cost effective, technologically backed, post pandemic preparedness program in the present market.
Our product design is based on an extensive study of guidelines from Govt of India, ICMR, CDC, WHO, etc

What Makes our Product Unique?

Our dynamic training techniques designed by healthcare professionals impart accurate updated safety protocols toteachers & students with easy to follow methods.
Our campus modifications & guidelines further make it easier to follow safety protocol so that schooling can be the primary focus while ensuring safety.
